Core Climate Threats

Fossil Fuel Addiction

🪓

Rapid Deforestation

Forests are our planet's natural carbon sinks. Slashing them accelerates global warming and wipes out crucial biodiversity.

The Throw-Away Culture

We can't sustain a take-make-dispose model on a finite planet. Transitioning to a zero-waste, circular economy is essential.

🧵 The "Circular Wardrobe" 🧵

An interactive platform where users digitize their closets, log their clothing items, and get matched with local tailors or upcycling workshops in the Delhi/NCR region.

Upcycling Engine

Users input items they no longer wear. The web app uses gamification to suggest DIY alteration ideas or connects them to local, verified tailors to give clothes a second life.

SDG 12 Impact

Promotes "Reduce, Reuse, Repair" directly targeting fast fashion and textile waste.
